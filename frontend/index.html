Understanding the Parameters

Pregnancies: Number of times pregnant. Essential context for gestational diabetes.
Glucose (mg/dL): Normal fasting range 70-99. Over 125 indicates diabetes. Vital energy source.
Blood Pressure (mmHg): Ideal is <120/80. Diastolic pressure is commonly measured.
Skin Thickness (mm): Triceps skin fold thickness. Normal range is typically 10-30 mm.
Insulin (µU/mL): Normal fasting 2.6-24.9. Hormone that regulates blood sugar.
BMI: Healthy weight is 18.5-24.9. Over 30 is considered obese. Evaluates body fat.
Diabetes Pedigree: A score assessing diabetes likelihood based on family genetic history.
Age (Years): Risk generally increases with age, especially post-45 years.
HbA1c (%): Measures average blood sugar over 3 months. Normal range is below 5.7%.
